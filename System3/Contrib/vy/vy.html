Project Voyager

Voyager is a project to explore the feasibility of a portable and extensible system for simulation and data analysis systems. The Voyager project is carried out by StatLab Heidelberg. All components of Voyager are intended to be open for the public domain.


  Voyager is distributed from statlab.uni-heidelberg.de . Beta software and later will be available via anonymous ftp. For access to development and alpha software, contact G. Sawitzki <gs@statlab.uni-heidelberg.de>. 
Selected contributions from Voyager are available by ftp from ETH Zürich.
